# Audityzer Pricing

## Overview
Audityzer offers flexible pricing tiers designed to meet the needs of businesses of all sizes, from startups to enterprises.

## Pricing Tiers

### Free Tier
- **Price**: $0/month
- **Features**:
  - Up to 1,000 audits per month
  - Basic reporting
  - Email support
  - Community access

### Pro Tier
- **Price**: $29/month (billed monthly) or $290/year (billed annually - save 17%)
- **Features**:
  - Up to 50,000 audits per month
  - Advanced reporting with custom branding
  - Priority email & chat support
  - API access
  - Custom integrations
  - Team collaboration (up to 5 users)

### Enterprise Tier
- **Price**: Custom pricing (contact sales)
- **Features**:
  - Unlimited audits
  - Dedicated account manager
  - 24/7 phone & email support
  - On-premise deployment option
  - Custom SLA agreements
  - Advanced security features
  - Unlimited team members

## Additional Features

### Optional Add-ons
- **Extra Audit Slots**: $0.01 per audit (beyond plan limits)
- **Premium Support**: $99/month
- **Advanced Analytics**: $49/month
- **White-label Solution**: $199/month

## Billing & Payment
- All plans are billed in USD
- Monthly plans can be canceled anytime
- Annual plans include a 17% discount
- 30-day money-back guarantee
- Multiple payment methods accepted (credit cards, bank transfer, PayPal)

## FAQ

**Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?**
A: Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle.

**Q: Do you offer discounts for long-term commitments?**
A: Yes! Annual billing includes a 17% discount. Contact our sales team for custom enterprise pricing.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: Yes! Start with our Free Tier with no credit card required. Upgrade anytime to access more features.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my plan limits?**
A: We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. You can purchase additional audit slots at $0.01 per audit or upgrade your plan.
